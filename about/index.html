About me.

I'm a self-taught 3D artist from Frankfurt am Main, Germany.
I got my hands on blender about five years ago to try and make custom scenery objects for NoLimits2. My first attempts were... rough, but ~4,000h later I'd consider myself pretty damn good.
Early on I joined a small dev team to make NoLimits2 maps and quickly took the role of lead 3d artist thanks to my attention to detail and overall model quality. I leared a good bit of game design theory as well, though I prefer modelling. I left the team in February 2021 due to abuse and extreme pressure/time demands with lacking or nonexistent compensation.

Until mid-2022 I mostly did projects alone in my free time. Then I joined a separate group of people working on-and-off on a game to help out with modelling and texturing.
On the side I've also picked up character modelling and VRChat world creation. I've now made a handful of characters and published them as VRChat avatars for friends or commissioners.
So. Here I am now with plenty of experience under my belt.

What are my skills?

> I can do hardsurface- and poly-modelling.
> I can accurately model complex organic shapes with good topology.
> I can easily stick to a limit for poly-count (within reason).
> I can make clean and space-efficient UVMaps
> I can do simple rigging and skinning. (think VRChat-grade. not movie-grade.)
> I can make good-looking and detailed PBR textures.
> I can adapt to and stick with a project's artstyle. Be it toon or realism.

What do I struggle with?

> Sculpting. I haven't had the chance to properly learn it and much prefer hardsurface.
> Insufficient references. I'm a perfectionist and not knowing what something looks like exactly slows my modelling process down quite a bit.
> Artblock. I'm not the most creative person, but I can get the job done if I'm told what to make.

Other things I dabble in occasionally.

> Web developement (guess who made this website)
> Programming - I can write and read simple c# code, though my knowledge is still lacking in some regards.
> Music - both mixing and producing. I like hardtechno and jungle dnb a lot.
> 2D Art - I can draw... sort of. I'm definitely more confident with 3D. It helps for texturing though!
> Rhythm Games - they're cool.
