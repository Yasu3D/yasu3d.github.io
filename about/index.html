About me.

I'm a self-taught 3D artist from Frankfurt am Main, Germany.
I got my hands on blender about five years ago to try and make custom scenery objects for NoLimits2. My first attempts were... rough, but ~4,000h later I'd consider myself pretty damn good.
Early on I joined a small dev team to make NoLimits2 maps and quickly took the role of lead 3d artist thanks to my attention to detail and overall model quality. I leared a good bit of game design theory as well, though I prefer modelling. I left the team in February 2021 due to abuse in the team.
Until mid-2022 I mostly did projects alone in my free time. I joined a separate group of people working on-and-off on a game to help out with modelling and texturing.
On the side I've also picked up modelling Anthro Characters for VRChat and had a Hand at making my own VRChat world(s).
So. Here I am now. With plenty of experience under my belt.

What are my skills?

> I can do hardsurface- and poly-modelling.
> I can accurately model complex organic shapes with good topology.
> I can easily stick to a limit for poly-count (within reason).
> I can make clean and space-efficient UVMaps
> I can do simple rigging and skinning. (think VRChat-grade. not movie-grade.)
> I can make good-looking and detailed PBR textures.
> I can adapt to and stick with a project's artstyle. Be it toon or realism.
