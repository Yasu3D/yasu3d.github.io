Commission Info

I'm currently not open for commissions.

I offer commissions for:
> custom-made furry character models.
> simple assets (furniture, props, etc.)

Pricing

simple assets

> starting at 10€, anything goes. pricing for simple assets varies greatly depending on the request. contact me and I'll give you a quote.

characters

> starting at 200€. I may charge more for complex character designs or multiple outfits.

Terms of Service

general

> commissioning me means you agree with my TOS and abide by them.
> breaking these TOS will get you on a public blacklist stating why you're blacklisted.
> I have every right to deny or cancel a commission at any moment.

payment

> payment is done in € [EURO] over PayPal.
> depending on the project, I either charge 100% upfront or 50% upfront/50% after finishing.
> refunds are decided on a case-by-case basis. If you wish to cancel a commission the amount you are refunded depends on how much work has already been done.

copyright [CC Licenses]

Assets
> assets will be distributed under a CC BY-SA license unless stated otherwise.
commercial and derivative works are allowed, as long as you're not blatantly reselling the original model.

Characters
> characters will be distributed under a CC BY-NC-ND license unless stated otherwise.
> you're still welcome to ask for small changes after the commission is finished and I'll make them free of charge. If you want to make a major edit yourself, please contact me about it.

references

references must be clear and unambiguous. I don't enjoy making changes because of an outdated or confusing reference.
In case anything should differ from a reference, please clearly state the difference and either provide alternate references or describe it in detail.
