yasu

yasu is a small introverted housecat [ragdoll breed].
he's rather quiet, but will warm up to people if they get to know him.

color/design

body is light-cream.
lower limbs have a gradient to medium-brown starting at elbow/knee.
toes and fingers are dark-brown.
tail has a gradient over medium-brown to dark-brown.
ear inside is light-cream, outside is medium-brown.
dark-brown tiger stripes on back over a medium-brown background.
messy, slightly reddish-brown hair.

body

male body, very fluffy.
young adult, no exact age.
165cm / 5'5".
70kg / 154lbs.
slightly chubby, bmi of ~26.
slightly lower-than-average strength.

social/behavior

very honest and blunt.
quiet, especially in bigger groups.
very affectionate if he really likes someone.
purrs a *lot*.
