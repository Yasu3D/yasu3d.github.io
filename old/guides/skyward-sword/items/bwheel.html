slingshot

    info

    the slingshot is the first item obtained in skyward sword. getting it unlocks the B-Wheel.
    it's given to link by the kikwi elder 'bucha' in chapter 2 'search for zelda'

    the slingshot uses deku seeds as ammo, which can be stored in a seed satchel.
    it's base capacity is 20 shots.

    upgrades

    the slingshot has 1 upgrade, the scattershot. If charged up, it fires a wide
    volley of shots at the same ammo cost as a normal shot.

    version materials price location

bugnet

    info

    the bugnet is the second item link can obtain.
    it is not required to beat the game but you're missing out if you skip it.
    it can be bought in beedle's shop after obtaining the slingshot,
    which allows you to ring the shop's bell.

    upgrades

    the bugnet has 1 upgrade, the big bugnet.
    it is twice the size of the normal bugnet,
    making bug-catching much easier.

    version materials price location
    bugnet [none] 50 rupees beedle's shop
    big bugnet 3x tumbleweed
    2x ancient flower
    1x evil crystal    		 100 rupees scrap shop

beetle

    info

    the beetle is the third item link can obtain.
    it is found in chapter 2 'search for zelda' inside the skyview temple.

    upgrades

    the beetle has 3 upgrades. one major upgrade - the hook beetle -
    allows it to grab bigger items, such as bombs. further upgrades
    improve it's speed and flight distance.

    version materials price location

bombs

    info

    bombs are the fourth item link can obtain.
    they're found in chapter 2 'search for zelda' inside the earth temple.
    bomb flowers occur naturally in the world and can be plucked to refill your bomb bag.

    upgrades

    the bombs cannot be upgraded, instead only it's container is upgraded.


gust bellows

    info

    gust bellows are the fifth item link can obtain.
    they're found in chapter 2 'search for zelda' inside lanayru mining facility.
    they can blow dust away and spin certain wind turbines.

    upgrades

    the gust bellows cannot be upgraded.


whip

    info

    the whip is the sixth item link can obtain.
    it's found in chapter 3 'sacred flames' inside the ancient cistern.

    it can be whipped around special hooks to swing on, pull switches/valves
    and steal items off of enemies.

    upgrades

    the whip cannot be upgraded.


clawshots

    info

    the clawshots are the seventh item link can obtain.
    they're found in chapter 3 'sacred flames' as the reward for completing the
    lanayru desert silent realm.

    they can hook onto vines or special targets and pull link towards them.
    since they come in a pair link can hang on one target and hook onto another.

    upgrades

    the clawshots cannot be upgraded.


bow

    info

    the bow is the eighth and final item obtained in skyward sword.
    it's found in chapter 3 'sacred flames' on the sandship.

    the bow uses arrows as ammo, which can be stored in a quiver.
    it's base capacity is 20 shots.

    upgrades

    the bow has 2 upgrades, the iron bow and sacred bow,
    which both improve it's power and range.
