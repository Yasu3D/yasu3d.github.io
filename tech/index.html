Audio

I'm not quite an audiophile, but I do like me some nice audio tech. I don't have some huge wall of headphones or an entire bag of IEMs, just a handful of stuff I enjoy (or don't).

The ranking list

These are my opinions. Ranks are mostly relative to each other, not absolute. They'll change with new things I try. I'm also ranking all kinds of audio in one list because I like both apples and oranges, alright?

Value stars are a bit different to crinacle's.
x = do not buy
* = diminishing returns
** = worth the money
*** = good value

Numbers in [brackets] under ranks are total tuning, tech and comfort scores. The lower, the better.
Think S+ = 1, S = 2, etc. Then add them up for final score and average for the rank.
